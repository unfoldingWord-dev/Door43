===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For that which I do, I do not really understand** - "I am not sure why I do some of the things that I do". (See: [[:en:ta:translate:hyperbole]])
  * **For** - "I do not understand why I do what I do because"
  * **what I hate, I do** - Alternate translation: "The things that I know are not good are the things that I do."
  * **But** - "However"
  * **I agree with the Law** - Alternate translation: "I know God's law is good."
