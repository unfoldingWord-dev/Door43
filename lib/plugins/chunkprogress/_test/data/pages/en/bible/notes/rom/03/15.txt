===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Their...their...They...their** - the "Jews and Greeks" of [[:en:bible:notes:rom:03:09|3:9]]
    * **Their feet are swift to pour out blood** - "They are in a hurry to harm and kill people" (See: [[en:ta:translate:Synecdoche]])
    * **Destruction and suffering are in their paths** - "Everyone lives in such a way that they intentionally try to destroy others and cause them to suffer" (See: [[en:ta:translate:Metonymy]])
    * **a way of peace** - A "way" is a "road" or "path." Alternate translation: "how to live at peace with others" (See: [[en:ta:translate:Metaphor]])
    * **There is no fear of God before their eyes** - "Everyone refuses to give God the respect he deserves" (See: [[en:ta:translate:Synecdoche]])
