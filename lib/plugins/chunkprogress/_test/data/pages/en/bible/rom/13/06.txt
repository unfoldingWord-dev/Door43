===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Because of this** - "Because the government punishes evildoers" 
  * **you....Pay to everyone** - Paul is addressing the believers here. (See: [[en:ta:translate:formsofyou|]])
  * **For** - "This is why you should pay taxes:"
  * **attend to** - "administer" or "work on"
  * **toll** - customs duty
