===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **If the Spirit...lives in you** - Paul assumes that the Holy Spirit lives in his readers. Alternate translation: "Since the Spirit...lives in you."
  * **of him who raised** - "of God, who raised" 
  * **mortal bodies** - "physical bodies" or "bodies, which will die someday"
