===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For Christ is the fulfillment of the Law**  - "For Christ completely fulfilled the Law"
  * **for righteousness for everyone who believes **  - "in order that he may make everyone who believes in him right before God"
  * **the righteousness that comes from the Law**  - "how the Law makes a person right before God"
  * **The man who does the righteousness of the Law will live by this righteousness**  - "The person who perfectly obeys the Law will live because the Law will make him right before God."
  * **will live** - This can refer to 1) eternal life (see UDB) or 2) mortal life in fellowship with God.
